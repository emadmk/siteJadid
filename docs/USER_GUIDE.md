## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click **Sign Up** in the navigation
2. Choose your account type:
   - **Personal (B2C)**: For individual customers
   - **Business (B2B)**: For wholesale purchases
   - **Government (GSA)**: For government agencies
3. Fill in your information
4. Verify your email
5. Complete your profile

### Signing In

1. Click **Sign In**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

## Account Types

### B2C (Consumer)

**Features:**
- Standard retail pricing
- Loyalty points on purchases
- Product reviews
- Wishlist
- Order tracking

**Pricing:**
- Base retail price
- Sale prices when available
- Loyalty tier discounts

### B2B (Business)

**Features:**
- Wholesale pricing
- Bulk order discounts
- Net payment terms (Net 30/60)
- Purchase order support
- Dedicated account manager
- Credit limit management

**How to Apply:**
1. Create account
2. Submit business verification:
   - Company name
   - Tax ID
   - Business license
3. Wait for approval (1-2 business days)
4. Start ordering at wholesale prices

**Pricing:**
- Wholesale prices (typically 15-30% off retail)
- Volume discounts
- Tier-based pricing

### GSA (Government)

**Features:**
- GSA contract pricing
- GSA SmartPay accepted
- Compliance documentation
- Government purchase orders
- Contract-specific terms

**Requirements:**
- Valid government email
- Agency information
- Contract number
- Vendor ID

## Shopping

### Browsing Products

1. **Browse by Category**: Click categories in navigation
2. **Search**: Use search bar for specific products
3. **Filter**: Use filters to narrow results:
   - Price range
   - Category
   - Features
   - Availability

### Product Details

Each product page shows:
- Product images
- Description and specifications
- Pricing (based on your account type)
- Stock availability
- Customer reviews
- Related products

### Adding to Cart

1. Select product options (if available)
2. Choose quantity
3. Click **Add to Cart**
4. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

### Wishlist

Save products for later:
1. Click heart icon on product
2. View wishlist from account menu
3. Move items to cart when ready

## Checkout Process

## Loyalty Program

### Tiers

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Bronze | $0 spent | 1% rewards |
| Silver | $1,000 spent | 3% rewards |
| Gold | $5,000 spent | 5% rewards |
| Platinum | $10,000 spent | 7% rewards |
| Diamond | $25,000 spent | 10% rewards |

### Earning Points

- Earn 1 point per $10 spent
- Bonus points on promotions
- Birthday bonus
- Review products for points

### Redeeming Points

1. Go to checkout
2. View available points
3. Choose points to redeem
4. Discount applied automatically

### Tier Benefits

Higher tiers receive:
- Higher reward percentages
- Exclusive discounts
- Early access to sales
- Free shipping thresholds
- Priority customer service

## Order Management

### Viewing Orders

1. Go to **My Account** → **Orders**
2. View order history
3. Click order for details

### Order Status

- **Pending**: Payment processing
- **Confirmed**: Payment received
- **Processing**: Being prepared
- **Shipped**: On its way
- **Delivered**: Completed
- **Cancelled**: Order cancelled

### Tracking Orders

1. Open order details
2. Click tracking number
3. View real-time tracking

### Returns & Refunds

**Return Policy:**
- 30 days from delivery
- Items must be unused
- Original packaging required

**How to Return:**
1. Go to order details
2. Click **Request Return**
3. Select items and reason
4. Print return label
5. Ship items back
6. Refund processed within 5-7 days

### FAQs

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Go to My Account → Orders and click on your order to see tracking.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Credit/debit cards, PayPal, bank transfer (B2B), net terms (B2B), GSA SmartPay (GSA).

**Q: How do I return an item?**
A: Request a return from your order details within 30 days of delivery.

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Standard shipping: 5-7 business days. Expedited: 2-3 business days.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Currently, we only ship within the United States.

**Q: How do loyalty points work?**
A: Earn 1 point per $10 spent. Redeem points for discounts at checkout.

**Q: How do I become a B2B customer?**
A: Sign up for a B2B account and submit your business verification documents.

**Q: What is the minimum order for B2B?**
A: Minimum order varies by product. Check individual product pages.
