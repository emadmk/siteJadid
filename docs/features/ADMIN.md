## Dashboard
- Overview statistics

- Pending approvals
- Revenue charts

### Products List
- View all products
- Search & filter
- Bulk actions
- Export to CSV

- Pricing (Base, Sale, Wholesale, GSA)
- Inventory tracking
- Images & videos
- Categories
- Attributes
- Variants
- SEO metadata
- Compliance certifications

### Create/Edit Product
- All fields editable
- Image upload (multiple)
- Video upload
- Category assignment
- Attribute assignment
- Status control (Draft/Active/Inactive)

### Inventory Management
- Stock quantity per warehouse
- Low stock threshold
- Reorder point
- Inventory adjustments
- Transfer between warehouses
- Inventory history log
